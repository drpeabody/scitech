Cephied

The Astronomy Club

The astronomy club of IIT Hyderabad is a club which seeks to create an atmosphere for fostering and nurturing the interest of the students regarding astronomy and astrophysics. It aims to expand the horizons of the students and develop a celestial passion among the students who seek knowledge in this field. The club helps the students to develop a better understanding of these mysterious forces in nature and what governs them. The major motivation of the club is to gather together all the enthusiasts of astronomy and work together; so that we may understand and help others understand the innate beauty of the universe.

The following is a list of Club Activities:

  • Astronomy Quiz
  • Celestial Inquisition
  • Discussion Sessions
  • Observation Sessions

The Electronics Club

he Electronics Club aims at teaching and helping people understand the seemingly incomprehensible electronic gadgets in the world today, and also assists people in developing their own devices. It strives to create interest in the students for electronics. ELECTRONIKA was launched by a few students of IITH last year, with a vision to build a club that strives to create interest in electronics to all students.

Kludge

The InfoSec Club

The Information Security club of IIT Hyderabad was started up with a vision to equip people to combat in what is now emerging as a new battleground - The Internet! Kludge, the name means " quick, easy yet effective solutions to the toughest problems in the world" and this is exactly what the club aims to equip people to do. "Kludge" aims at creating an aware society, one that can use ethical hacking to improve the defensive techniques for the latest technology and even foray into Cyber Forensics and create Security Professionals for the development and improvement in the field of Information Security.

Robotics

The Robotics Club

The robotics club is one of the earliest clubs started in the budding years of IITH. With the perseverance and enthusiasm of its members, through the years it has flourished into a fully functional club with a simple goal to expose the students to the ever wondrous field of robotics. Armed with the support of the institute, the knowledge pool and bubbling enthusiasm of its members, robotics club aims to provide the opportunity and materials to harness the creativity of the students and provide a tangible proof of their ingenuity. Currently club has around 25 consistent members from second years. Club will take new members from first years after ηvision 2012.

Torque

The Automobile Club

Torque, a club started by some automobile aficionados, is aimed to nourish the passion of automobile enthusiasts. The members, referred as "petrol-heads" work together to organise and participate in various sessions to spread the culture and develop the interest among the "a-bit-interested" people.

The following is a list of Club Activities:

  • Torque Design Challenge
